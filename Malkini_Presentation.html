दूध का हिसाब (Milk Tracker)

Never Miscalculate the Milk Bill Again

  • Log precise amounts (e.g., 1.5 Liters) with specific dates.
  • Month-end automatic calculation based on your custom rate.
  • Instant addition to monthly expenses once cleared.

प्रेस के कपड़े (Laundry Tracker)

Keep Track of Every Shirt and Saree

  • Log exact clothing descriptions effortlessly (e.g., "4 shirts, 3 jeans").
  • Date-based tracking ensures nothing gets lost or forgotten.
  • Easy 2-step verification and clearance at the end of the month.

Shopping & Expenses

  • Dynamic Shopping List: Add items and check them off with one tap (Pending ➔ Purchased).
  • Expense Tracker: Manually add custom expenses.
  • Monthly Insights: See your total household spend automatically calculated for the current month.
